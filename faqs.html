General Questions

GAI is a nonprofit organization dedicated to providing education, healthcare, disaster relief, and community development support to vulnerable communities worldwide.

You can donate directly through our Donate Page, selecting the project or cause you want to support. We accept one-time and recurring donations.

We work across multiple countries, focusing on regions facing crises such as poverty, natural disasters, and lack of basic resources.

Every donation is allocated to the chosen project, covering essential needs like food, clean water, education, healthcare, and emergency aid.
